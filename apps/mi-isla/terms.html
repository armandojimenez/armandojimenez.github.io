2. App Description

Mi Isla is a boricua-first app that brings together what is happening in Puerto Rico in one place: weather, official alerts, earthquakes and tsunami status, Sahara dust, air quality, the sea and beaches, rivers and flooding, and precipitation radar. It is bilingual (Spanish and English) and free at its core, with an optional Premium subscription.

  • Platforms: iOS and Android.
  • Availability: Provided on an "as available" basis. Features depend on the public data services the App relies on.
