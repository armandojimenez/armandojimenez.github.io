2. Privacy-First Approach

Monea is built on a privacy-first foundation. We believe your manifestation practice and personal data should remain private. Unlike many apps, we:

  • Store all data locally on your device by default
  • Never upload your user data to our servers
  • Don't require email or login
  • Store your display name locally only

Important: If you delete the app, all your data will be permanently lost. We cannot recover it because we never had access to it.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Create

All of the following data is stored locally on your device and NOT transmitted to our servers:

  • Deposit Entries: Virtual currency amounts and dates
  • Purchase History: Catalog items bought with virtual currency
  • Custom Display Name: Your chosen name within the app
  • Deposit Settings: Mode, frequency, and amounts
  • Collection Data: Owned items and card customizations
  • Notification Preferences: Your reminder settings
  • Achievement Progress: Milestones and badges earned

We may collect the following anonymous data to improve the app experience:

  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reports for stability improvements.

10. Your Privacy Rights

10.1 GDPR Rights (European Union)

If you are in the EU, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request information about data collected
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

10.2 CCPA Rights (California)

If you are in California, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: Request details about collected data
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of data sale (we do not sell data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

10.3 Exercising Your Rights

Since most data is stored locally on your device:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app at any time
  • Deletion: Delete your data in Settings
  • Export: Export your data through the app
