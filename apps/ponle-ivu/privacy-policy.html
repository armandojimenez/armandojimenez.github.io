1. Introduction

Welcome to Ponle IVU ("the App"), developed and operated by Believe PR, owned by Armando Jimenez. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information.

Ponle IVU is a tax calculator application designed to help you calculate taxes (IVU/IVA), tips, discounts, and split bills among multiple people. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

Key Privacy Principles:
  • All calculations are performed locally on your device
  • No personal calculation data is transmitted to our servers
  • No user accounts or registrations required
  • Minimal data collection for app functionality only

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Ponle IVU operates entirely offline. The app does NOT collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Financial information (credit card details, bank accounts)
  • Calculation data (purchase amounts, tax calculations, receipts)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Photos or media files

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

For free users, Google AdMob may automatically collect limited technical information for advertising purposes:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • Usage Data: App interactions, ad impressions, ad clicks
  • Technical Data: IP address (approximate location only), crash logs for stability

Note for Premium Users: Users who purchase the ad-free version will not have any data collected by Google AdMob.

2.3 Saved Receipts

When you save receipts within the app:

  • All receipt data is stored locally on your device only
  • Receipt data is never uploaded to external servers
  • You have full control to view, edit, or delete saved receipts
  • Receipt data is included in device backups (iCloud/Google Drive) if enabled

3. How We Use Your Information

Since Ponle IVU operates entirely offline, we do not directly collect or use your personal information. However, third-party services may use collected data for:

3.1 App Functionality

  • Performing tax, tip, and discount calculations locally on your device
  • Saving and managing your receipts within the app
  • Generating PDF reports of your calculations
  • Maintaining app preferences and settings

3.2 Advertising (Free Version Only)

Google AdMob uses collected data to:

  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Prevent fraud and ensure ad quality
  • Provide personalized ads based on your interests (with your consent)

3.3 App Improvement

  • Analyzing crash reports to fix bugs and improve stability
  • Understanding app usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Testing new features and improvements

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your calculation data and saved receipts are stored exclusively on your device using:

  • iOS: Secure app sandbox storage protected by iOS security features
  • Android: Internal app storage with Android security protections

4.2 Device Backups

Your saved data may be included in device backups:

  • iOS: iCloud Backup (if enabled by you)
  • Android: Google Drive Backup (if enabled by you)

These backups are controlled by Apple and Google's respective privacy policies, not by us.

4.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • App code is regularly updated and security-tested
  • No data transmission means no interception risk
  • Local device security protections (encryption, biometric authentication)
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

4.4 Data Retention

  • Local data remains on your device until you delete the app
  • Deleting the app will permanently remove all saved calculations and receipts
  • Premium purchase records are retained by Apple/Google for transaction purposes

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have specific privacy rights:

7.1 GDPR Rights (European Union)

  • Right to Access: Request information about data collected
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

7.2 CCPA Rights (California)

  • Right to Know: Request information about collected data
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of data sale (we do not sell data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

7.3 Exercising Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Deletion: Delete individual receipts in the app or uninstall to remove all data
  • Export: Use the PDF export feature to save your data
  • Advertising: Use device settings to manage ad preferences
