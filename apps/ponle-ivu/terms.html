2. App Description

2.1 Purpose

Ponle IVU is a calculator application designed to help users calculate:

  • Sales tax (IVU, IVA, or other applicable taxes)
  • Tips for service
  • Discounts on purchases
  • Bill splitting among multiple people
  • Total purchase amounts

2.2 Key Features

  • Tax Calculation: Add tax to purchase amounts
  • Tip Calculator: Calculate service tips by percentage or amount
  • Discount Calculator: Apply percentage or fixed discounts
  • Bill Splitting: Divide totals among multiple people
  • Receipt Management: Save and organize calculations
  • PDF Export: Generate PDF reports of calculations
  • Offline Functionality: All features work without internet connection
