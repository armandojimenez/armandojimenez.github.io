# Ponle IVU - Privacy Policy & Terms

This folder contains the privacy policy and terms & conditions pages for the **Ponle IVU** mobile application.

## App Information

- **Name:** Ponle IVU
- **Type:** Tax Calculator (IVU/IVA/Tax + Tips + Discounts + Bill Splitting)
- **Platforms:** iOS & Android
- **Developer:** Armando Jimenez / Believe PR
- **Features:**
  - Calculate sales tax (IVU/IVA)
  - Calculate tips
  - Apply discounts
  - Split bills among multiple people
  - Save receipts locally
  - Export calculations as PDF
  - 100% offline functionality
  - No data synced or saved outside the app

## Monetization

- **Free Version:** Full features with Google AdMob advertisements
- **Premium Version:** One-time purchase to remove ads permanently
- **No Subscriptions:** All features available without recurring payments
