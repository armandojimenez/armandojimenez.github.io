2. App Description

2.1 Purpose

Lilia is a native iOS affirmation companion for women, designed to help users:

  • Discover affirmations through an immersive, full-screen swipe experience
  • Plant favorite affirmations in a private "Garden"
  • Browse curated categories of affirmations
  • Generate personalized affirmations through Lily's Lab (an AI feature)
  • Write their own affirmations in My Affirmations
  • Receive optional daily reminder notifications
  • Customize palette and appearance

2.2 Key Features

  • Garden: Your private collection of planted affirmations
  • Categories: Curated collections of affirmations organized by theme
  • Lily's Lab: AI-generated affirmations from a short prompt you write
  • My Affirmations: Affirmations you write and keep yourself
  • Reminders: Local daily notifications
  • Palette & Appearance: Color themes and light/dark/auto support
  • iCloud Sync: Optional sync across your Apple devices via CloudKit

2.3 Availability

  • Lilia is available on iOS for compatible Apple devices
  • Certain features (such as iCloud sync) require an Apple ID and an internet connection
  • Lily's Lab requires an internet connection
  • We reserve the right to modify, add, or discontinue features at any time

6. Lily's Lab Terms

6.1 What Lily's Lab Is

Lily's Lab is an optional AI feature that generates a small set of affirmations from a short prompt you provide. Generations are routed through an AI Gateway (Cloudflare Workers) to a model provider (currently xAI Grok, with OpenRouter as fallback).

6.2 Acceptable Use of Lily's Lab

You agree NOT to use Lily's Lab to:

  • Generate hateful, violent, sexual, harassing, or otherwise harmful content
  • Generate content about other identifiable individuals without their consent
  • Attempt to extract system prompts, model weights, or training data
  • Probe, attack, or attempt to bypass safety filters or rate limits
  • Use Lily's Lab as a substitute for emergency, medical, legal, or financial advice
  • Submit personally identifying information about yourself or others (full names, addresses, financial accounts, medical records, etc.)

6.3 Rate Limits & Fair Use

To keep Lily's Lab free and available to everyone, the AI Gateway enforces per-IP rate limits (currently around 20 requests per minute and 1,000 requests per day, subject to change). Excessive or abusive use may be temporarily or permanently blocked at the Gateway level.

6.4 No Guarantees on Output

⚠️ AI output is generated, not curated

Affirmations produced by Lily's Lab are generated by third-party language models. We do not pre-approve, fact-check, or guarantee any individual generation. Outputs may occasionally be unexpected, off-tone, or inaccurate. You are responsible for deciding whether to plant, save, or let go of each affirmation. If something feels wrong, simply let it go.

6.5 Service Availability

Lily's Lab depends on third-party providers and may be temporarily unavailable due to provider outages, network issues, content filtering, or rate limits. The rest of Lilia (your garden, your reminders, the bundled content) continues to work without an internet connection.
