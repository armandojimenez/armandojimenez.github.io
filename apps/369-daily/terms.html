2. App Description

2.1 Purpose

369 Daily is a manifestation and mindfulness application based on the 369 method — inspired by Nikola Tesla's fascination with the numbers 3, 6, and 9. The App is designed to help users:

  • Practice the 369 writing method — write your affirmation 3 times in the morning, 6 times in the afternoon, and 9 times in the evening
  • Build a consistent daily manifestation ritual through structured repetition
  • Cultivate mindfulness, intentionality, and personal growth
  • Track progress and maintain motivation over 33-day and 45-day manifestation cycles

2.2 Key Features

  • 369 Writing Method: Guided sessions for writing affirmations 3 times in the morning, 6 times in the afternoon, and 9 times in the evening
  • Manifestation Cycles: 33-day and 45-day structured manifestation programs
  • Session-Aware Themes: Dynamic color themes that change based on time of day (morning, afternoon, evening)
  • Smart Notification Reminders: Timely reminders to complete each writing session
  • Progress Calendar & Streak Tracking: Visual calendar and streak system to track your consistency
  • Daily Reflection Prompts: Thought-provoking prompts to deepen your practice
  • Share Affirmation as Image: Export and share your affirmations as beautifully designed images
  • Bilingual Support: Full support for English and Spanish

  • No user accounts are required to use the App
  • Your affirmations and personal data never leave your device
