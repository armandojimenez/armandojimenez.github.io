1. Introduction

Welcome to 369 Daily ("the App"), developed and operated by Believe PR, owned by Armando Jimenez. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy.

369 Daily is a manifestation app based on the 369 method, inspired by Nikola Tesla's fascination with the numbers 3, 6, and 9. The app guides you through a daily writing ritual: write your affirmation 3 times in the morning, 6 times in the afternoon, and 9 times in the evening. All affirmations and personal data remain entirely on your device.

✨ Our Privacy Promise:
  • All your data stays on YOUR device
  • We CANNOT access your affirmations or entries
  • No user accounts or email required
  • Your manifestation practice is completely private
  • Zero analytics or tracking

2. Privacy-First Approach

369 Daily is built on a privacy-first foundation. We believe your manifestation practice, affirmations, and personal reflections should remain entirely private. Unlike many apps, we:

  • Store all data locally on your device using a local database
  • Never upload your affirmations, entries, or progress to any server
  • Don't require email, login, or any user account
  • Use zero analytics services -- no Firebase, no Crashlytics, no tracking whatsoever
  • Include no advertising of any kind

Important: If you delete the app, all your data will be permanently lost. We cannot recover it because we never had access to it.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Create

All of the following data is stored locally on your device and is NOT transmitted to any server:

  • Affirmations: The manifestation text you write during 3-6-9 sessions
  • Session Progress: Completion status for morning, afternoon, and evening writing sessions
  • Cycle Data: Your manifestation cycle progress (33 or 45 day cycles)
  • Streak & Calendar Data: Daily completion history and streak counts
  • Daily Reflections: Personal reflections and journal entries
  • Notification Preferences: Your reminder schedule and settings
  • Theme Preferences: Session-aware theme and appearance choices
  • Language Preference: Your chosen language (English or Spanish)

3.2 Information Automatically Collected

369 Daily does NOT automatically collect any data. We use:

  • No analytics services -- zero usage tracking
  • No crash reporting services -- no crash data is transmitted
  • No remote configuration -- all settings are local

3.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect or have access to:

  • Your affirmations or manifestation text
  • Your session history or writing entries
  • Personal identification (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Health data
  • Social media profiles or contacts
  • Device usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Advertising identifiers

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 App Functionality (On-Device)

Your data is used entirely on your device to:

  • Guide you through 369 writing sessions (3x morning, 6x afternoon, 9x evening)
  • Track your manifestation cycle progress (33 or 45 day cycles)
  • Display your streak count and progress calendar
  • Send local session reminders at your preferred times
  • Apply session-aware themes (morning, afternoon, evening color palettes)
  • Generate shareable affirmation images
  • Show daily reflection prompts

4.2 What We Do NOT Use Your Data For

Because we have zero access to your data:

  • We do NOT analyze your affirmations or entries
  • We do NOT use your data for advertising
  • We do NOT sell or share your data with anyone
  • We do NOT use your data for analytics or improvement

Your affirmations and entries never leave your device.

5. Data Storage & Security

All your personal data is stored on your device using:

  • Database: Local SQLite/Drift database
  • iOS: Secure app sandbox with iOS encryption
  • Android: Internal app storage with Android security
  • Data remains on your device indefinitely until you delete it
  • Deleting the app removes all local data permanently
  • No cloud backup or sync -- your data exists solely on your device

9. Your Privacy Rights

9.1 GDPR Rights (European Union)

If you are in the EU, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request information about data collected
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

9.2 CCPA Rights (California)

If you are in California, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: Request details about collected data
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of data sale (we do not sell data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

9.3 Exercising Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app at any time
  • Deletion: Delete your data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in device settings
  • Control: You have full control over all your data since it never leaves your device

Note: Because we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, most privacy rights are automatically fulfilled by our privacy-first design.
