2. App Description

2.1 Purpose

Unworried is a wellness application designed to help users build the personality trait of being cool under pressure. The App allows you to:

  • Describe situations that are causing you worry or stress
  • Receive AI-generated fresh perspectives to help shift your thinking
  • Build a journal of perspectives for reflection
  • Track daily streaks to build a consistent practice
  • Save favorite perspectives for quick access
  • Share perspectives with others
  • Export your journal as CSV

2.2 Key Features

  • Perspective Generation: AI-powered fresh perspectives on your situations
  • Journal: Browse and reflect on past perspectives
  • Streaks: Daily activity tracking to build consistency
  • Favorites: Save and revisit meaningful perspectives
  • Sharing: Share perspectives via native share sheet
  • Export: Export your journal data as CSV
  • Local Storage: All data stored privately on your device
  • Bilingual: Available in English and Spanish

2.3 Availability

  • The App is available for iOS and Android devices
  • Certain features may vary by platform
  • An internet connection is required for AI perspective generation
  • We reserve the right to modify or discontinue features at any time
