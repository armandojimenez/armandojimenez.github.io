2. App Description

2.1 Purpose

Devotion is an AI-powered daily devotional app designed for Christians. It provides a personalized spiritual experience by generating daily devotional content that includes scripture, reflections, prayers, affirmations, and action steps.

2.2 Features

  • Daily Devotional Generation: One AI-generated devotional per day, personalized to your mood and chosen category
  • Personalization: Optional mood text entry and category selection (Gratitude, Peace, Hope, Strength, Healing, Joy, Forgiveness, Purpose, Patience, Guidance)
  • Devotional History: Browse and re-read all your past devotionals
  • Bookmarks: Save your favorite devotionals for quick access
  • Daily Reminders: Customizable push notifications for your devotional practice
  • Light & Dark Mode: Full theme support for comfortable reading

2.3 Availability

  • Platforms: iOS and Android
  • Internet Required: Active internet connection is required to generate new devotionals
  • Offline Access: Previously generated devotionals are available offline
