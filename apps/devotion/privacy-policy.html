1. Introduction

Welcome to Devotion ("the App"), developed and operated by Believe PR, owned by Armando Jimenez. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and your spiritual practice.

Devotion is an AI-powered daily devotional app for Christians. Each day, the app generates a personalized devotional experience that adapts to your mood, chosen category, and the historical and spiritual significance of the date. Content is generated via Google's Gemini AI through Firebase AI Logic.

2. Privacy-First Approach

Devotion is built on a privacy-first foundation. We believe your spiritual practice and personal reflections should remain private. Unlike many apps, we:

  • Store all devotional data locally on your device
  • Never upload your personal content to our servers
  • Don't require email, login, or any personal identification
  • Don't serve any advertisements

Important: If you delete the app, all your saved devotionals, bookmarks, and preferences will be permanently lost. We cannot recover this data because we never had access to it.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Create

All of the following data is stored locally on your device and is NOT transmitted to our servers:

  • Generated Devotionals: Daily AI-generated devotional content including scripture, reflections, prayers, and affirmations
  • Mood & Category Selections: Your optional mood text and chosen devotional category (e.g., Gratitude, Peace, Hope)
  • Bookmarked Devotionals: Devotionals you choose to save for later reading
  • Devotional History: A chronological record of all your past devotionals
  • Notification Preferences: Your daily reminder settings and times
  • Theme Preferences: Light mode, dark mode, or system theme selection

3.2 Information Automatically Collected

We may collect the following anonymous data to improve the app experience:

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics such as screen views, feature usage, and your selected devotional category (e.g., "Hope", "Peace"). No personally identifiable information is collected. We do NOT collect the free-text mood description you enter.
  • Firebase Remote Config: Feature flags and app configuration. No personal data is collected.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Anonymous crash reports including device type and OS version for stability improvements. Your devotional content is never included.
  • Firebase App Check: Device attestation tokens to protect our backend from abuse. No personal data is collected.

3.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect or have access to:

  • ‚ùå Your mood entries or personal feelings
  • ‚ùå Your devotional content or reflections
  • ‚ùå Personal identification (name, email, etc.)
  • ‚ùå Location data or GPS information
  • ‚ùå Health or biometric data
  • ‚ùå Contacts, calendar, or social media profiles
  • ‚ùå Financial or banking information

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 App Functionality (On-Device)

Your data is used entirely on your device to:

  • Display your daily devotional content
  • Store and retrieve your devotional history
  • Manage your bookmarked devotionals
  • Send daily devotional reminders at your chosen time
  • Apply your preferred visual theme

4.2 AI Content Generation

When you generate a devotional, the following is sent to Google's Gemini AI:

  • Your optional mood text (if provided)
  • Your selected category (if chosen)
  • The current date for contextual content

This information is used solely to generate your personalized devotional. See Section 6 for details on AI privacy.

4.3 App Improvement

Anonymous data helps us:

  • Fix bugs via anonymous crash reports
  • Understand feature usage via anonymous analytics
  • Optimize app performance and stability

Your devotional content and personal entries are NEVER used for analytics.

5. Data Storage & Security

All your personal data is stored on your device using:

  • Database: Local SQLite database via Drift (an offline-first database)
  • Preferences: SharedPreferences (platform-standard key-value storage) for theme and notification settings
  • iOS: Secure app sandbox with iOS encryption
  • Android: Internal app storage with Android security

5.1 Data Retention

  • Data remains on your device indefinitely until you delete it
  • Deleting the app removes all local data permanently
  • We have no ability to recover deleted data

6. AI-Generated Devotionals

6.1 How It Works

Devotion uses Google's Gemini AI (via Firebase AI Logic) to generate daily devotional content:

  • Your optional mood text and chosen category are sent to Google's servers for processing
  • The AI generates a complete devotional including scripture, reflection, prayer, and an action step
  • The generated content is delivered to your device and stored locally

6.2 Privacy in AI Processing

Important privacy protections:

  • Your mood input and category selections are processed by Google Gemini but are NOT stored long-term by Google
  • Inputs are not linked to your personal identity
  • No conversation history is retained on Google's servers after generation
  • Subject to Google's AI privacy policies
üí° Tip: We strongly recommend you do not include personal names, locations, or other identifying information in your mood entry. The mood text you type is sent to Google's servers for AI processing.

6.3 Important Disclaimers

‚ö†Ô∏è AI Content Disclaimer

Devotion's AI-generated content is:

  • For spiritual enrichment and personal reflection only
  • Not a substitute for pastoral guidance, counseling, or professional advice
  • Not endorsed by any specific church, denomination, or religious authority
  • Generated content should be considered alongside your own Scripture study and community of faith

6.4 Opting Out

The AI generation is a core feature of the app. You can choose not to provide a mood or category, in which case the AI will generate a general devotional for the day.
