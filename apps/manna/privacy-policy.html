1. Introduction

Welcome to Manna ("the App"), developed and operated by Believe PR, owned by Armando Jimenez. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy.

Manna is a Christian affirmations app designed to help you gather daily truth through Scripture-inspired affirmations. The app provides daily affirmations across 20 categories, customizable reminders, personal affirmation creation, and visual themes to enhance your spiritual practice.

üçû Our Privacy Promise:
  • All your data stays on YOUR device
  • We CANNOT access your personal affirmations or entries
  • No user accounts or email required
  • No analytics, tracking, or third-party data collection
  • Your spiritual practice is completely private

2. Privacy-First Approach

Manna is built on a privacy-first foundation. We believe your spiritual practice and personal affirmations should remain entirely private. Unlike many apps, we:

  • Store all data locally on your device only
  • Never upload your user data to any servers
  • Don't require email, login, or any personal information
  • Include zero analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • Operate fully offline — no internet connection required for core functionality

Important: If you delete the app, all your data will be permanently lost. We cannot recover it because we never had access to it. Use the in-app backup feature to save your data before uninstalling.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Create

All of the following data is stored locally on your device and is NEVER transmitted to our servers or any third party:

  • Affirmations: Built-in and custom affirmations you create (in English and/or Spanish)
  • Favorites: Affirmations you bookmark for quick access
  • Custom Mixes: Category combinations you create
  • Custom Themes: Visual themes with colors, fonts, and background images
  • Hidden Affirmations: Content you choose to hide from your feed
  • Reminder Settings: Notification schedules, times, days, and preferred categories
  • Backup Files: Encrypted JSON exports of all your data

3.2 Usage Data Stored Locally

The following usage data is stored on your device only for app functionality (e.g., streak tracking, review prompts) and is NEVER transmitted externally:

  • Session Data: Total sessions, days used, and last session date
  • Engagement Data: Swipe counts, favorites count, share count
  • Streak Data: Current streak and activity dates
  • Preferences: Theme mode, language selection, display settings

3.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect or have access to:

  • ❌ Your affirmations, favorites, or custom content
  • ❌ Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ Location or GPS data
  • ❌ Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • ❌ Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • ❌ Health, financial, or contact data
  • ❌ Photos (we access only images you explicitly select)

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 App Functionality (On-Device Only)

Your data is used entirely on your device to:

  • Display your daily affirmation feed from selected categories
  • Manage your favorites and hidden affirmations
  • Send scheduled local notification reminders
  • Apply your chosen visual themes and display preferences
  • Track your engagement streak for motivation
  • Enable backup and restore of your personal data
  • Power the search feature across all affirmations

4.2 What We Do NOT Use Data For

We do NOT use any data for:

  • Advertising or ad targeting
  • Analytics or usage tracking
  • Profiling or behavioral analysis
  • Sale or sharing with third parties
  • Marketing communications

5. Data Storage & Security

All your personal data is stored on your device using:

  • Database: Local SQLite/Drift database (manna.sqlite)
  • Preferences: SharedPreferences for settings
  • Images: App document directory for custom theme images
  • iOS: Secure app sandbox with iOS encryption
  • Android: Internal app storage with Android security

5.1 Data Retention

  • Data remains on your device indefinitely until you delete it
  • Deleting the app removes all local data permanently
  • Backup files you export are under your control

5.2 Backup & Restore

Manna provides a backup and restore feature:

  • Backups are stored as JSON files in the app documents directory
  • Maximum backup file size: 10 MB
  • Backups contain all your affirmations, settings, themes, and preferences
  • Backup files are stored locally and never uploaded to any server
  • You are responsible for safeguarding any exported backup files

6. Permissions

6.1 Required Permissions

Notifications

  • Purpose: Deliver scheduled affirmation reminders
  • Privacy: Notifications are generated locally and do not transmit data
  • Control: You can disable notifications at any time in device settings

Local Storage

  • Purpose: Save app data, themes, and backup files on your device

6.2 Optional Permissions

Photo Library Access

  • Purpose: Select images for custom theme backgrounds and mix cover images
  • What We Access: Only photos you explicitly select through the image picker
  • Storage: Selected images are copied to the app directory and stored locally

Save to Photo Library

  • Purpose: Save affirmation screenshots to your photo library for sharing
  • Control: Only triggered when you explicitly share or save an affirmation

6.3 Permissions We DON'T Request

Manna does NOT request:

  • ❌ Location/GPS
  • ❌ Contacts
  • ❌ Calendar
  • ❌ Health data
  • ❌ Microphone
  • ❌ Camera (we use the system photo picker only)
  • ❌ Bluetooth
  • ❌ Internet access for data collection

7. Third-Party Services

Manna is designed to operate without third-party services. We do NOT integrate:

  • ❌ Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, or any Google services
  • ❌ Google AdMob or any advertising service
  • ❌ Facebook SDK or any social media tracker
  • ❌ Any analytics or tracking platform
  • ❌ Any cloud storage or sync service
  • ❌ Any payment processing service

7.1 Platform Services

The only external interactions are through standard platform features:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: App distribution and updates
  • In-App Review: Native OS review prompt (no data is sent to us)
  • Share Sheet: System share functionality when you choose to share an affirmation
  • URL Launcher: Opens external links (developer website, Instagram) in your default browser

These are standard operating system features and are governed by Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.

No Advertising Services: Manna does NOT use Google AdMob or any other advertising service. There are no ads in the app.

8. Your Privacy Rights

8.1 GDPR Rights (European Union)

If you are in the EU, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: All your data is accessible within the app at any time
  • Right to Rectification: Edit or correct any data directly in the app
  • Right to Erasure: Delete any or all data through the app settings
  • Right to Data Portability: Export your data using the backup feature
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing (though we process no data externally)
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Revoke permissions at any time in device settings

8.2 CCPA Rights (California)

If you are in California, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: We collect no personal information beyond what is stored on your device
  • Right to Delete: Delete all data by removing the app or using in-app settings
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell any data
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

8.3 Exercising Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app at all times
  • Deletion: Delete individual items or all data through the app
  • Export: Use the backup feature to export all your data
  • Permissions: Manage all permissions through your device settings

9. Children's Privacy

Manna is intended for a general audience aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

  • Minimum age: 13 years old
  • Parental supervision recommended for younger users
  • No personal data is collected from any user regardless of age

10. International Users

10.2 Regional Compliance

  • California: CCPA compliant

  • Other Regions: We respect all applicable privacy laws
