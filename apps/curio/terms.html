2. App Description

2.1 Purpose

Curio is an AI-powered fact discovery application designed to help users:

  • Discover interesting facts about any topic they type
  • Choose from five presentation tones: Curious, Playful, Scholarly, Witty, or Poetic
  • Browse their history of past fact discoveries
  • Share fascinating facts with others

2.2 Key Features

  • AI Fact Generation: Enter any topic and receive 1-3 interesting facts
  • Tone Selection: Customize how facts are presented with five distinct tones
  • Fact History: Browse and search past discoveries with date grouping
  • Share Facts: Share interesting discoveries with friends and family
  • Dark Mode: Light, dark, and automatic theme options
  • Local Storage: Fact history stored privately on your device

2.3 Availability

  • The App is available for iOS and Android devices
  • An internet connection is required for fact generation
  • Certain features may vary by platform
  • We reserve the right to modify or discontinue features at any time
