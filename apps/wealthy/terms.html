2. App Description

2.1 Purpose

Wealthy is a native iOS affirmation companion focused on wealth, abundance, and money mindset, designed to help users:

  • Discover affirmations through an immersive, full-screen swipe experience
  • Save favorite affirmations to a private "Vault"
  • Browse curated categories of money, abundance, and wealth-identity affirmations
  • Generate personalized affirmations through Mint (an AI feature)
  • Write their own affirmations in My Favorites
  • Receive optional daily reminder notifications
  • Customize palette and appearance

2.2 Key Features

  • Vault: Your private collection of saved affirmations
  • Categories: Curated collections of affirmations organized by theme (Daily Wealth, Abundance, Wealth Identity, Manifesting, Money Confidence, Earning Power, and more)
  • Mint: AI-generated affirmations from a short prompt you write
  • My Favorites: Affirmations you write or favorite for yourself
  • Reminders: Local daily notifications
  • Palette & Appearance: Color themes and light/dark/auto support
  • Widget: Daily Wealth card surfaced on your Home Screen
  • iCloud Sync: Optional sync across your Apple devices via CloudKit
  • Wealthy Vault (paid tier): Optional subscription that unlocks the full library and additional Mint capacity

2.3 Availability

  • Wealthy is available on iOS for compatible Apple devices
  • Certain features (such as iCloud sync, Mint, and Wealthy Vault) require an Apple ID and an internet connection
  • We reserve the right to modify, add, or discontinue features at any time
