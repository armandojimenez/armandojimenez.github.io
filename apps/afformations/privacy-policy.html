1. Introduction

Welcome to Afformations ("the App"), developed and operated by Believe PR, owned by Armando Jimenez. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy.

Afformations is an empowering questions app based on the Afformations methodology. It helps you build a daily practice of asking better questions across 21 categories of personal growth, with AI-powered question generation, custom themes, and bilingual support (English and Spanish) — all while keeping your data private and secure on your device.

Our Privacy Promise:
  • All your data stays on YOUR device
  • No user accounts required
  • No advertisements, no ad tracking
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No subscriptions or in-app purchases
  • We CANNOT access your personal data

2. Privacy-First Approach

2.1 Our Philosophy

Afformations is built on a privacy-first foundation. We believe your personal growth journey should remain private. Unlike many apps, we:

  • Store all data locally on your device
  • Never require user accounts or email addresses
  • Do not display advertisements
  • Do not collect analytics or usage data
  • Do not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way
  • Cannot read your favorites, custom afformations, or any personal content

2.2 What This Means for You

When you use Afformations:

  • Your favorite afformations remain on your phone
  • Your custom afformations never leave your device
  • Your theme preferences stay local
  • Your practice history is stored only on your device
  • Your reminder settings stay on your device

Important: If you delete the app, all your data will be permanently lost. We cannot recover it because we never had access to it.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Create

All of the following data is stored locally on your device and NOT transmitted to our servers:

  • Favorites: Afformations you mark as favorites
  • Custom Afformations: Questions you write yourself
  • Practice History: Your daily deck review history
  • Mixes: Custom collections you create
  • Theme Preferences: Your chosen visual themes and custom themes
  • Reminder Settings: Notification times and preferences
  • App Settings: Language, appearance mode, deck size preferences

3.2 Information Sent to AI Services

When you use the "Create with AI" feature:

  • What is sent: Your prompt text, selected style, tone, and categories
  • Where it goes: Google Gemini via Firebase AI Logic SDK
  • What is NOT sent: Your favorites, history, custom afformations, or any other personal data
  • Purpose: Solely to generate personalized afformations based on your request

See Section 6 for full details on AI data handling.

3.3 Information Collected for Support

Only when you choose to contact us via the in-app "Contact Us" feature:

  • Device Info: Device model, OS version (via device_info_plus)
  • App Info: App version, build number (via package_info_plus)
  • App Language: Your selected language setting
  • Basic Stats: Number of favorites saved, number of custom afformations

This information is included in the support email only when you explicitly choose to send it. It is never collected automatically.

3.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect or have access to:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device advertising identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Your favorites, history, or custom afformations
  • Health data
  • Contacts, calendar, or social media data
  • Financial or payment information

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 App Functionality (On-Device)

Your data is used entirely on your device to:

  • Display your daily deck of afformations
  • Show your favorites and practice history
  • Present your custom afformations and mixes
  • Apply your chosen visual theme
  • Send local notification reminders
  • Search across your afformation library

4.2 AI Feature

When you use the AI generation feature, your prompt is sent to Google Gemini solely to generate afformations. The generated results are stored locally on your device. See Section 6 for details.

4.3 Feature Flags

We use Firebase Remote Config to manage feature flags (enabling/disabling features remotely). This service does not collect personal data or track individual users.

5. Data Storage & Security

5.1 Local Storage

All your personal data is stored on your device using:

  • Database: Local SQLite database (via drift)
  • Preferences: SharedPreferences for settings
  • iOS: Secure app sandbox with iOS encryption
  • Android: Internal app storage with Android security

5.2 Encryption

  • At Rest: Your device's built-in encryption protects stored data
  • In Transit: AI requests use HTTPS/TLS encryption

5.3 Data Retention

  • Data remains on your device indefinitely until you delete it
  • You can delete individual favorites, custom afformations, or history entries at any time
  • Deleting the app removes all local data permanently
  • There is no cloud backup — your data exists only on your device

6. AI-Generated Afformations

6.1 How AI Works in Afformations

Afformations uses the Firebase AI Logic SDK to connect to Google Gemini (Gemini 2.0 Flash Lite) for generating personalized afformations. Here's how it works:

  • User-Initiated: AI generation only happens when you explicitly tap "Generate"
  • What is Sent: Your prompt text, selected style, tone, and category preferences
  • What is Returned: A set of generated afformation questions
  • Local Storage: Generated afformations you choose to save are stored locally

6.2 Privacy in AI Processing

  • Only the specific prompt and settings for each generation request are sent
  • Your favorites, history, custom afformations, and other app data are never sent
  • We do not use your prompts to train AI models
  • Generation requests are processed by Google under their Privacy Policy
  • There is a daily generation limit to prevent excessive API usage

6.3 Disclaimers

AI Content Disclaimer

AI-generated afformations are:

  • For personal growth and self-reflection purposes only
  • Not a substitute for professional advice (medical, psychological, or otherwise)
  • Generated content that may not always be perfectly aligned with your intent
  • Not verified or endorsed by the app developers

9. Your Privacy Rights

9.1 GDPR Rights (European Union)

If you are in the EU, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: All your data is accessible within the app at any time
  • Right to Rectification: Edit your custom afformations and settings directly
  • Right to Erasure: Delete any data within the app, or delete the app entirely
  • Right to Data Portability: Your data is stored locally and accessible on your device
  • Right to Object: You control all data processing through app features
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time by adjusting permissions or deleting the app

9.2 CCPA Rights (California)

If you are in California, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: This policy details all data practices
  • Right to Delete: Delete your data through the app or by uninstalling
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell personal data
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

9.3 Exercising Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Access: Open the app to view all your data
  • Deletion: Delete individual items or uninstall the app
  • Control: Manage all permissions through device settings

For questions about third-party data (like Firebase), contact us at: afformations@armandojimenez.dev
