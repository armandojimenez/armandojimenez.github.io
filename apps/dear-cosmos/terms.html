2. App Description

2.1 Purpose

Dear Cosmos is a cross-platform (iOS and Android) reflective companion that lets you:

  • Ask the universe a question and receive an answer written in its own first-person voice
  • Choose a voice (tone) that shapes how the answer feels
  • Watch a living, tone-reactive cosmos and receive a small cosmic sign with each answer
  • Keep your questions and answers in a private journal, "Your Cosmos"
  • Receive an optional gentle daily message
  • Add a home-screen widget that surfaces your latest sign

2.2 Key Features

  • Ask: A question, a chosen voice, and an answer composed in real time
  • Your Cosmos: Your private, on-device journal of past questions, answers, and signs
  • Cosmic Sign: A small symbol and affirmation that accompanies each answer
  • Share: An optional, beautifully rendered card you can share from your device
  • Daily Message: One gentle local notification, if you turn it on
  • Widget: Your latest sign on the home screen
  • Appearance: Deep Void and Daybreak themes, both languages (English and Spanish)

2.3 Availability

  • Dear Cosmos is available on iOS and Android
  • Asking a question requires an internet connection. Your journal, your saved answers, the daily message, and the widget continue to work offline
  • We reserve the right to modify, add, or discontinue features at any time
