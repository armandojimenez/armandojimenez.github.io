1. Introduction

Welcome to Refract ("the App"), a daily light companion developed and operated by Believe PR, owned by Armando Jimenez. You bring Refract a mood, a topic, or a few words, and the prism refracts what you're carrying into the message you needed, delivered in one of six wavelengths (registers of care) and saved, if you wish, in your own collection.

Refract is built around a quiet belief: what you're carrying is yours alone. We treat what you bring with the same care we hope it gives back to you.

✦ Our Privacy Promise:
  • Refract does not require an account, an email, or a password
  • Your messages, your Future Self letters, and your settings live on YOUR device
  • There is no cloud account and no server copy of your collection
  • To compose a message, only the text of that one request and your chosen wavelength are sent to our AI Gateway
  • There are no advertisements, no analytics, and no tracking SDKs

2. Privacy-First Approach

2.1 Our Philosophy

The things people bring to Refract are personal, so Refract is built to ask for as little as possible. With Refract we:

  • ✓ Store all of your personal data locally on your device by default
  • ✓ Use no advertising networks, no Google Analytics, no Firebase, and no third-party trackers
  • ✓ Do not require user accounts, email addresses, or passwords
  • ✓ Send only the request you make (and the wavelength you picked) when you ask for a message, with no profile or history built about you
  • ✓ Keep no server-side copy of your collection, your Future Self letters, or your settings

2.2 What This Means for You

When you use Refract:

  • The requests you make and the messages you receive stay on your phone
  • The messages you keep, and the Future Self letters you seal, stay on your phone
  • Your theme, language, and reminder preferences stay on your phone
  • A daily reminder, if you turn it on, is scheduled locally by your device

Important: Because we never keep a copy of your collection, deleting Refract will permanently remove your kept messages and Future Self letters. We cannot recover them, because we never had them. Refract includes a free manual backup so you can export your own data and keep it safe (see section 5.5).

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Create (Stored On Your Device)

The following data is created by you and stored locally on your device:

  • Your collection (Keep): The messages you choose to keep, each with its wavelength and date
  • Future Self: The letters you write to your future self and seal for a chosen delivery date
  • Your own lines: Messages you write yourself with "Create your own"
  • Appearance & language: Your theme preference (System, Morning Light, or Quiet Evening) and language
  • Daily reminder: Whether you have turned on the gentle daily reminder, and at what time
  • Your light (streak & counts): Local counts such as your "days lit" streak, kept only on your device
  • First-run state: Whether you have completed onboarding

3.2 What a Request Sends

Asking the prism for a message is the only action that contacts a server. When you make a request, the App sends the following to our AI Gateway so it can compose a message:

  • The text of that one request (your mood, topic, or words) and the wavelength you selected (or that the prism chose)
  • A random device identifier (a string generated on your device, not your name, email, or any account) used only to apply fair-use rate limits
  • An app identifier and environment (for example, refract and prod) and a token that authorizes the App to use the Gateway
  • Your IP address, as is technically necessary for any internet request

The Gateway, hosted on Cloudflare Workers, forwards your request to a third-party AI model provider to compose the message you read. The provider and model are configured on the server side (currently the Qwen family of large language models) and may change to maintain quality, without an app update. The Gateway never receives your name, email, location, collection, Future Self letters, or any other personal information. It only sees the request for that one message.

3.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

Refract explicitly does NOT collect or have access to:

  • ✗ Your name, email address, phone number, or contacts
  • ✗ Your precise location or GPS data
  • ✗ A server-side copy of your collection, your Future Self letters, or your settings
  • ✗ Your photos, camera, microphone, or files (Refract can add a share image you create to your gallery, but never reads your photo library)
  • ✗ Health data or wellness metrics from any source
  • ✗ Real-world financial data: Refract does not connect to banks or any account
  • ✗ Social media identities, friend lists, or third-party login data
  • ✗ Advertising identifiers (IDFA or Google Advertising ID): Refract has no advertising
  • ✗ Analytics, behavioral, or usage-tracking data of any kind

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 App Functionality (On-Device)

Your data is used entirely on your device to:

  • Show the message for each request you make
  • Keep your collection of messages you chose to save
  • Hold your sealed Future Self letters until their delivery date
  • Apply your selected theme, language, and appearance
  • Schedule your local daily reminder, if you enabled it
  • Show today's light on the home-screen widget, if you added one
  • Count your "days lit" streak and gentle stats, on your device

4.2 Composing a Message

The request you submit is used solely to compose that one message and is not used for any other purpose. We do not retain it to train models, build a profile, or improve the service over time.

4.3 Operational Logs

The AI Gateway keeps minimal operational logs (timestamp, app identifier, environment, status code) for short-term abuse prevention and rate limiting. These logs are not linked to any user identity beyond an IP address and the random device identifier.

4.4 No Marketing, No Profiling

We do not use any of your information for marketing, profiling, behavioral advertising, or selling to third parties. There is no advertising in Refract.

5. Data Storage & Security

5.1 Local Storage

Your personal data is stored on your device using:

  • A local database inside the App's private storage sandbox, for your collection and Future Self letters
  • Device key-value storage for preferences (theme, language, daily reminder, onboarding)
  • Operating-system data protection, which encrypts app files at rest on modern iOS and Android devices

5.2 Encryption

  • At Rest: Data is protected by your device's file-system encryption, tied to your device passcode or lock
  • In Transit: Every request to compose a message uses HTTPS (TLS)

5.3 Security Practices

  • The Gateway enforces per-device and per-IP rate limits to mitigate abuse
  • Refract does not include any analytics, attribution, or crash-reporting SDKs
  • Refract does not embed third-party web views that load outside content into the experience

5.4 Data Retention

  • Your data remains on your device for as long as Refract is installed
  • You can delete individual messages and letters at any time inside the App
  • Deleting the App removes all locally stored data permanently
  • Your request is not retained by Refract or by the Gateway after the message is returned

5.5 Backup & Export (You Are In Control)

Refract includes a free manual backup. You can export your kept messages, your Future Self letters, and your settings to a portable file (a .refract JSON file) and import it back later or on another device. This export is created and shared by you, through your device. We never receive it, and it is never uploaded to us.

6. The AI Behind Your Light

6.1 What It Is

When you make a request, the message is composed by a large language model and shaped by the wavelength you chose. You decide whether to keep each message, share it, or let it go. Future Self letters and your own lines can be gently polished by the same model if you ask for it.

6.2 How It Works

  • You bring a mood, a topic, or a few words, and pick a wavelength in the App
  • Refract sends that request and wavelength to our AI Gateway over HTTPS
  • The Gateway (Cloudflare Workers) forwards it to a third-party AI model provider (currently the Qwen family of models, configured server-side)
  • The model returns a message, which Refract displays to you
  • The request is not retained, indexed, or used to build a profile

6.3 A Safety Net That Stays On Your Device

Before any request is sent, Refract runs a small on-device check for signs of crisis or self-harm. This check runs entirely on your phone. If it is triggered, Refract does not send your text to the Gateway; instead it shows supportive, human help resources (see section 16). In other words, text that looks like a crisis never leaves your device.

6.4 Privacy in AI Processing

  • ✓ No name, email, or account is attached to the request. The Gateway has no idea who you are
  • ✓ Your collection and Future Self letters are NEVER sent
  • ✓ The Gateway does not retain prompt text after a response is returned
  • ✓ The model provider processes the request to generate a response in real time
  • ⚠ The model provider receives only the request you typed and the wavelength you chose. Treat it like any other AI assistant input

6.5 What Not to Share

⚠ A gentle reminder

Because your request is sent to an AI provider to compose your message, please do not include information you would not type into any other AI assistant, for example full names of other people, home addresses, bank or card numbers, government IDs, or medical record numbers. Refract is here to listen to what is on your heart, not to collect documents.

6.6 Not Professional Advice

Refract is for reflection, encouragement, and a moment of light. It is a creative companion, not a substitute for professional advice. Its messages are not predictions, fortune-telling, or medical, mental-health, legal, or financial advice. If you are facing a real decision or a hard moment, please reach out to a qualified professional or one of the resources listed in section 16.

7. Subscriptions & Payments

7.1 Refract Premium

Refract is free to use, with a generous free tier. Refract Premium is an optional subscription that unlocks unlimited refractions, an unlimited collection, a longer Future Self horizon, wallpaper exports, and other premium touches. Free dark mode and the free manual backup are never gated.

7.2 How Billing Works

  • The store handles payment. All purchases are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play. Believe PR never sees or stores your card or payment details
  • RevenueCat manages entitlements. We use RevenueCat to check whether your subscription is active. RevenueCat receives an anonymous subscriber identifier and the receipt the store provides; it does not receive your name, email, or your in-app content
  • No account needed. Premium is tied to your store account (Apple ID or Google account) through the store's "Restore Purchases" mechanism, not to a Refract account

7.3 Prices, Trial & Renewal

  • Refract Premium is offered as a monthly or yearly plan, with an optional free trial on the yearly plan. The exact, localized price and any trial length are always shown in the App and at checkout, may change over time, and may vary by region
  • Subscriptions renew automatically until cancelled. You can manage or cancel anytime in your device's store subscription settings; the App links you there
  • Refunds are handled by Apple or Google under their policies, not by Believe PR

8. Notifications & Widgets

8.1 The Daily Reminder (Local Only)

Refract can send a gentle daily reminder if you choose to turn it on. These are local notifications scheduled by your device. There is no push-notification server, no token tied to an account, and no message travels through Believe PR's infrastructure to reach your device.

8.2 Future Self Delivery (Local Only)

When a Future Self letter reaches its delivery date, Refract notifies you with a local notification scheduled by your device. The letter itself was on your device the whole time; nothing is sent to or from a server to deliver it.

8.3 The Home-Screen Widget

If you add the Refract widget to your home screen, it shows today's light or a quiet prompt to refract again. The widget reads a small snapshot stored locally on your device. It does not make its own network requests and does not transmit anything off your device.

8.4 You Are Always In Control

You can turn the daily reminder on or off inside Refract, and you can disable notifications entirely in your device's system settings for the App. You can remove the widget at any time from your home screen.

11. Your Privacy Rights

11.1 GDPR Rights (European Union / UK)

If you are in the EU, EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the following rights under the GDPR and UK GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request information about data processed about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format (the App's free export already provides this)
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your data
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: With your local data-protection authority

11.2 CCPA / CPRA Rights (California)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know, delete, and correct personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share your personal information as those terms are defined under the CPRA.

11.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

Because almost all of your data lives on your own device:

  • Access & Portability: Open Refract. Your collection and letters are visible inside the App at any time, and you can export them to a file
  • Correction & Erasure: Delete messages and letters directly inside the App, or delete the App to remove all local data
  • Other Requests: Email refract@armandojimenez.dev with your request

We will respond to formal rights requests within the timeframe required by applicable law (generally within 30 days).
