2. App Description

2.1 Purpose

Refract is a cross-platform (iOS and Android) reflective companion that lets you:

  • Bring a mood, a topic, or a few words, and receive a message composed in real time
  • Choose one of six wavelengths (registers of care, such as an affirmation, a spark of courage, a practical tip, or a gentle reminder), or let the prism choose for you
  • Keep the messages that land, in your own private, on-device collection
  • Write your own line, optionally polished by the App's AI feature
  • Write letters to your future self and seal them for a chosen delivery date
  • Share a beautifully rendered card, and optionally save it to your device
  • Receive an optional gentle daily reminder, and add a home-screen widget

2.2 Key Features

  • Refract: A request, a chosen wavelength, and a message composed in real time
  • Keep: Your private, on-device collection of messages you saved
  • Future Self: Letters you write now and seal for later delivery
  • Create your own: Write your own line, with optional AI polish
  • Share: An optional, beautifully rendered card you can share or save from your device
  • Daily Reminder & Widget: A gentle local reminder and today's light on your home screen
  • Appearance: Morning Light and Quiet Evening themes, both languages (English and Spanish)

2.3 Availability

  • Refract is available on iOS and Android
  • Composing a new message requires an internet connection. Your collection, your Future Self letters, "Create your own," the daily reminder, the widget, and backup continue to work offline
  • We reserve the right to modify, add, or discontinue features at any time
