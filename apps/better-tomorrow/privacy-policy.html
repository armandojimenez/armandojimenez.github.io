1. Introduction

Welcome to Better Tomorrow ("the App"), developed and operated by Believe PR, owned by Armando Jimenez. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and mental wellness data.

Better Tomorrow is a mood tracking and journaling application designed to help you understand your emotional patterns, journal your thoughts, capture moments with photos and voice recordings, and gain AI-powered insights - all while keeping your most personal data private and secure on your device.

üå∏ Our Privacy Promise:
  • All your mood data, journals, photos, and recordings stay on YOUR device by default

2. Privacy-First Approach

2.1 Our Philosophy

Better Tomorrow is built on a privacy-first foundation. We believe your thoughts, feelings, and personal moments should remain private. Unlike many apps, we:

  • ‚úÖ Store all data locally on your device by default
  • ‚úÖ Process AI insights on-device when possible
  • ‚úÖ Never upload your journals or moods to our servers
  • ‚úÖ Give you full control over cloud backups
  • ‚úÖ Don't require user accounts or email addresses
  • ‚úÖ Can't read your journal entries or access your photos

2.2 What This Means for You

When you use Better Tomorrow:

  • Your mood logs remain on your phone
  • Your journal entries never leave your device (unless you enable backup)
  • Your photos are stored in your local app storage
  • Your voice recordings stay on your device
  • AI pattern analysis happens locally (when supported)

Important: If you delete the app without backing up, all your data will be permanently lost. We cannot recover it because we never had access to it.

7. Optional Cloud Backup

7.1 How Cloud Backup Works

You have the option to backup your data to your own cloud storage:

  • iOS: iCloud (linked to your Apple ID)
  • Android: Google Drive (linked to your Google account)

Important: When you enable cloud backup, your data is uploaded to YOUR cloud account, not ours. We do not have access to your cloud storage.

7.2 What Gets Backed Up

When you enable cloud backup:

  • Mood entries and history
  • Journal entries and text
  • Photos (optionally)
  • Voice recordings (optionally)
  • Settings and preferences
  • AI insights and patterns

7.3 Backup Privacy & Security

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted before upload
  • Your Account: Stored in your personal cloud account
  • No Our Access: We cannot view or access your cloud data
  • Provider Policies: Subject to Apple iCloud or Google Drive privacy policies

7.4 Managing Backups

You can:

  • Enable or disable backup at any time in Settings
  • Choose what to backup (e.g., exclude photos/audio)
  • Manually trigger backups
  • Restore from backup when reinstalling
  • Delete cloud backups through your cloud provider

7.5 Backup Responsibility

Please note:

  • You are responsible for managing your cloud storage
  • Storage space used counts toward your cloud storage quota
  • We are not responsible for backup failures or data loss
  • Restoring from backup requires the same cloud account
